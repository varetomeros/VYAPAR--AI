**Vyapar AI** is a smart business assistant built for MSMEs to simplify daily operations and reduce manual work. It acts as a unified control layer that brings inventory, invoices, customers, and tasks into one system. Instead of using complex software, business owners interact with Vyapar AI using natural language, making technology easy to adopt. Real-time data visibility helps businesses respond faster, avoid errors, and improve cash flow. By automating routine processes and turning actions into structured insights, Vyapar AI enables small and medium businesses to operate efficiently, scale confidently, and focus more on growth rather than paperwork.
